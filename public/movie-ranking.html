The Best Movies According to Angela

My favorite movies of all-time.

Your Name. (君の名は。)

A beautifully animated Japanese film by Makoto Shinkai.

It tells the story of two teenagers who mysteriously swap bodies, leading to a touching narrative about fate, time, and connection.

Amélie

A whimsical French film about a shy young woman in Paris.

Through small acts of kindness, she changes the lives of those around her while discovering love and her own happiness.

Gone with the Wind

An epic historical romance set during the American Civil War.

It follows the strong-willed Scarlett O’Hara as she struggles with love, loss, and survival in a time of great upheaval.
